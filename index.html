BREAKING NEWS - Lipton changes its name once more

1 minute read • 1 April, 2023 04:20 UTC

The world’s largest Tea company, is changing its name once more. The privately owned company formerly known as ekaterra, then recently renamed to Lipton Tea & Infusions, is now changing its name once more to LIPPY, according to a source close to the matter. Speaking on the condition of anonymity, the source said that the name reflects a spirit of “Love, Laughter, Fun, Cheekiness, and not taking life too seriously”. The source also hinted that further details will be announced after midday today.
